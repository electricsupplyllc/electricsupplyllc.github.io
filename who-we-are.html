A long history in the electrical industry

Stephen Breland began working in the electrical construction industry over 33 years ago, before being promoted to a purchasing agent position after 6 years of dedicated service. He was passionate about his work within the field, and in 1994 he was offered an inside sales position with one of the electrical distributors he worked with.

 

After 25 years in various managerial and sales positions, Mr. Breland began to dream of owning his own distributorship. That dream is now a reality!

Locally owned and operated

After partnering with a valued customer and friend, Electric Supply was opened in Warner Robins to serve the customers of Georgia as a locally owned and operated business.

We are proud to serve a thriving community, and have the opportunity work independently as a distributor, free of outside influence.

Dedicated to our customers' needs

When we opened Electric Supply, our main goal was to create a business that focused in customer service.

We maintain that our customers are our greatest asset, and we will do whatever it takes to provide you with an exceptional experience with us.
